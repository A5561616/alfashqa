About Alfashqa Trading

Established in Ethiopia, Alfashqa Trading PLC is a premier import and export company headquartered in Addis Ababa with operations in Bahirdar. We take pride in connecting Ethiopia's rich agricultural heritage with global markets while facilitating essential imports to support our nation's growth.

Our expertise lies in sourcing, processing, and exporting premium Ethiopian commodities including world-renowned frankincense and gum, specialty coffee varieties, high-quality oil seeds, cereals, and therapeutic aromatic plants. We maintain rigorous quality standards to ensure our products meet international market expectations.
